At Futurewood, we are dedicated to providing you with quality, value-added alternatives to traditional wood.We think that looks great in a forest and ideally, it should stay there! That’s why we’ve made sure that there is a CleverDeck option for every budget and aesthetic. So out with your old decking and in with the new!
CleverDeck Xtreme
The CleverDeck Xtreme range is engineered to look the best and withstand the most intense weather. Made from cellulose materials and recycled virgin polyolefin plastic, the Xtreme range is tough beyond compare. Its hard outer shell is scratch-resistant, warp-proof, UV resistant, and virtually waterproof.

We offer a variety of colours in 2 widths, with a dynamic blended colour palette that looks like authentic wood. CleverDeck Xtreme decking is supplied in long 5.4m lengths for quicker installation with the option of custom made lengths for larger projects.

CleverDeck Eco-Pro is our lower cost composite decking option. It’s amazingly durable, available in one designer colour, and maintains the low maintenance promise of the CleverDeck composite decking range.
High-Quality Durable Decking For Your Outdoor Space
Unlike traditional wood decking that is cut from a tree, composite decking is made from primarily recycled materials. The Futurewood CleverDeck range is made from recycled plastic, rice husks, and hardwood sawdust. The CleverDeck Xtreme range also has a pure polymer shell over the recycled core. This allows us to create high-quality decking that is more cost effective over time and much easier to maintain.

The composite decking materials are also extremely durable. In the harsh Australian climate, having composite decking that is practically waterproof, scratch and fade-resistant, and super durable is a must.

Composite decking is an affordable option that lets you maintain your outdoor living space without sacrificing the quality or durability of your deck.

Why Choose Composite Decking?
CleverDeck Composite
Futurewood CleverDeck composite offers exceptional durability, providing protection from moisture and weathering. You’ll never have to sand, seal, stain, or paint your deck with harmful chemicals. With Futurewood CleverDeck, enjoy peace of mind and save time and money on ongoing maintenance.
Natural Wood
Natural wood decking is beautiful when first installed, but unlike composite, it’s vulnerable to the elements. Regardless of the wood species, you’ll need to sand, seal, stain, or paint every few years to maintain its appearance. Wood decks also attract insects and require eventual replacement.
Composite Decking Is Environmentally Friendly
Composite decking is an environmentally friendly alternative to timber. Production is more sustainable than timber farming and recycling materials reduces waste and pollution. So by choosing composite decking, you not only enjoy durable and low-maintenance decking. You also do your part in helping conserve the Australian ecosystem. And because we use recycled materials, we provide decking that is much more affordable in the long run compared to traditional wood options.
